SoundCloud Playlists


Helping listeners curate their playlists

This is a personal case study. This project and I are not affiliated with SoundCloud in any way.

The Problem

How might I improve the efficiency of playlist creation on SoundCloud’s mobile application?

Our Solution

I updated the UI to allow users to create playlists from their Collections tab.

Surveys revealed that most music streaming applications were used on their mobile phone. The top 2 use cases for streaming applications are “finding new music” and “creating playlists”

I sent out an online survey which garnered 36 responses. My goal for this survey was to verify two of my initial assumptions…

The majority of people who use music streaming platforms are accessing these services through mobile apps, rather than the desktop application.

A top use case for music streaming platforms are curating personal playlists.

While “finding new music” was the top use case, I decided to focus on improvements to playlist curation because I did not have the resources to simulate realistic/ecologically valid solutions to test. “Creating playlists” still garnered a large portion of the responses, and I would be able to create testable scenarios for my proposed solutions.

My updated user flow allowed users to create a playlist from their Collection tab.

As stated in the image above, the most common non-critical error was attempting to create a playlist from the playlist screen. I wanted to update the workflow to match users’ expectations. If they expect to create a playlist from that screen, they should be able to.

Follow-Up Usability Testing gave concrete, quantitative evidence that my updates lowered user drop-off in the playlist creation workflow.

To test my UI updates, I ran the same usability test, but with different participants.

Final Thoughts

While this project is relatively small in scope, it demonstrates the power of agile usability testing to identify significant improvements to a feature’s user experience. This project portrays my ability to setup evaluative research to drive design decisions. In this project, I wrote a detailed research plan, decided on quantitative criteria to test my redesign against, and found objective results to support my design decisions.

Moving forward, I would definitely love to tackle other features/use cases for the SoundCloud mobile app such as improvements to the iconography and discovery of new music.
